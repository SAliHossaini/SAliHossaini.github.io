About

My name is Sayed Ali Hossaini. I was born in Kabul, Afghanistan- and currently reside in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I graduated from the University of Isfahan in 2017 where I received a bachelor’s degree in Geomatics Engineering. I moved to Minnesota in 2019 to study Geography and Geographic Information Science at the University of Minnesota. My main interests are GIScience and Geovisualization applications in agriculture analytics, natural resources, and the environment. In my personal time, I enjoy biking, visiting museums, and gardening. In fact, I took the photo of the Cheetah pictured above while visiting the American Museum of Natural History in New York!
