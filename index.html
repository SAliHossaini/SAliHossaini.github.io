About

My name is Sayed Ali Hossaini. I was born in Kabul, Afghanistan- and currently reside in Minneapolis, Minnesota. I graduated from the University of Minnesota in 2022 where I received two Master's degrees in Geography and Geographic Information Science. I am an expert in creating, analyzing, mapping, and managing data using spatial data analytics packages in Python, SQL, and ArcGIS. My main interests are GIScience and Geovisualization applications in polar science, renewable energy, agriculture analytics, natural hazards, and the environment. In my personal time, I enjoy biking, visiting museums, and gardening. In fact, I took the photo of the Cheetah pictured above while visiting the American Museum of Natural History in New York!

GIS Analyst/Developer

I am looking for a career that is titled GIS Developer and fits my current skillset and my research background. I like to apply analytics and modeling aspects of GIS to solve real-world problems. I have honed my skills in imagery processing, geospatial data analyses, modeling, visualizing, cartography techniques, remote sensing, and geospatial artificial intelligence. I am passionate about applications of GIS in the solar industry, precision agriculture, hazards, and polar research.
